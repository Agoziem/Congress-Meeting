Count down to Students Congress 2022

From the Student's Corner:

After last year's Congress where we considered, "the cost of discipleship", Jesus began to emphasize that his disciples who has accepted to follow should press for more of his realities and power because he died for more. So I asked him, what do that mean and how do we come into these more that he is speaking about? In response he said that there are basic principles in the kingdom upon which the kingdom runs. He then revealed that the only way a man can enter into more of his realities and power is to prove Faithful in the little things that has been committed into his/her hand. So he demanded that we charge his people to be true and faithful in little, then the more will begin to come. ... Therefore, it is from this discussion that the theme for the congress was given as Faithful in little from Luke 19:17 and Luke (16:1-12). So we trust Jesus that he will open our eyes to look around us in those things in our hands already touching different aspects of life and making a conscious decision to be faithful in them.

Bro Chukwuka Nnaemeka
students Coordinator (ACM Awada Arch)
